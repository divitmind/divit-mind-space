Speech Therapy

At Divit MindSpace, we see communication as more than a skill—it is a child’s way of connecting with the world, expressing emotions, and building relationships.

We focus not just on how a child speaks, but on why they communicate, how they feel while doing so, and how communication can become meaningful for them.

We look at communication as a whole, including:

Speech clarity (articulation)
Understanding and using language
Fluency (flow of speech)
Social communication (verbal and non-verbal connection)

Our approach integrates these areas naturally into play, interaction, and everyday experiences—rather than isolating them into rigid drills.

By blending therapeutic expertise with a relationship-based, child-led approach, we help children move from pressure to comfort, and from hesitation to confident expression.

Is This the Right Space for Your Child?

This program is ideal for children facing:

Speech delays or limited speech
Non-verbal or very limited verbal communication
Unclear speech or pronunciation difficulties
Difficulty forming complete or grammatically correct sentences
Communication challenges associated with autism
Difficulty initiating or sustaining communication

Developmental Differences
