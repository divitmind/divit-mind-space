Assessment Hub: Mapping Your Unique Blueprint
An assessment is more than a diagnosis — it is a compassionate roadmap to understanding your inner world. By combining evidence-based clinical tools with an empathetic, neuro-affirming approach, we identify your strengths and pinpoint the precise support you need to thrive.
A) Assessment for Children & Adolescents
We take a holistic view of a child’s cognitive, emotional, and social development. We translate complex behaviors into clear, actionable insights that support success at home and school.

Autism: Beyond surface symptoms, we explore social communication, sensory profiles, and special interests to help the child feel truly seen and understood.
ADHD & Executive Function: We look beyond hyperactivity to assess attention, organization, impulse control, and emotional regulation, while differentiating ADHD from anxiety or learning differences.
Learning Disabilities (LD): Focused evaluations for reading, writing, and math to uncover the root causes of academic struggles.

Goal: To foster self-understanding, secure appropriate school accommodations, and create a personalized educational roadmap.

When to Reach Out for a Child or Adolescent Assessment:

Developmental gaps — missed milestones in speech, social interaction, or motor skills
Academic roadblocks — high effort with low results or increasing resistance to school
Emotional volatility — frequent intense meltdowns or sudden withdrawal from hobbies and friends
Behavioral puzzles — repetitive behaviors, sensory sensitivities, or actions that are hard to understand
Recommendations from teachers or caregivers for additional learning support or accommodations

Assessment for Adults & Geriatrics
Understanding your past to empower your future.

Stress: We identify your specific triggers and help your nervous system move from survival mode to sustainable balance.
Anxiety: Support for OCD, Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety, and other patterns — providing clarity and practical regulation tools.
Depression: Compassionate support through depressive episodes and burnout, including specialized maternal mental health screening and postpartum guidance.
Adult Autism & Adult ADHD: Neuro-affirming assessments for late-identified individuals, helping differentiate neurodivergence from burnout or anxiety.
Trauma & Grief: Safe, trauma-informed evaluations that respect your pace and support recovery.
Substance Use & Addiction: Non-judgmental assessments to understand underlying patterns and co-occurring needs.
Geriatric Care (55+): Dignified assessment of memory, mood, cognitive changes, and functional abilities to support independence and emotional well-being.

Goal: To promote radical self-acceptance, develop effective workplace or daily strategies, and create personalized plans for long-term emotional well-being.

When to Reach Out for an Adult or Senior Assessment:

Unresolved trauma or grief — ready to process past experiences at your own pace
Persistent anxiety — intrusive thoughts, panic, or social fears that limit daily life
Habit awareness — wanting to understand substance use or addictive patterns without judgment
Chronic burnout — feeling stuck in survival mode where stress outweighs coping ability
Late-life clarity — seeking to understand lifelong patterns of Adult ADHD or Autism
Cognitive changes (55+) — noticing shifts in memory, mood, or independence
