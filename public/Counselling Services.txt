Our Therapeutic Modalities

Evidence-Based Foundations: Rooted in scientifically validated frameworks including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT).
Integrated Approach: We combine traditional talk therapy with somatic awareness and expressive arts therapy to support holistic healing — addressing the mind, body, and spirit for lasting transformation.

Is This the Right Support for You?
We provide specialized support for the following concerns:

Neurodivergence Support (ADHD, Autism & Learning Disorders): Neuro-affirming care that honors your unique brain wiring, strengthens executive functioning, and helps you build an authentic and fulfilling life.
Anxiety & Stress Management: Breaking the cycle of chronic worry, social anxiety, or high-functioning burnout using CBT, mindfulness, and nervous system regulation tools.
Depression: Gentle, compassionate support to process feelings of heaviness, low motivation, and isolation while rebuilding connection and purpose.
Trauma: Trauma-informed, safety-focused therapy that incorporates somatic awareness to process past experiences and restore inner security.
Expressive Arts Therapy: Creative healing through art, movement, and metaphor — ideal when words alone are not enough for emotional exploration and release.

Group Therapy: The Power of Shared Experience

Support Groups: Targeted groups for specific concerns such as postpartum challenges or neurodivergent adults.
Process Groups: Focused on interpersonal growth, relational patterns, and emotional processing.
Skill-Building Groups: Practical groups teaching DBT skills or social-emotional learning.
