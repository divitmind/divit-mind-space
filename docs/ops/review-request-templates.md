# Review-request & HARO templates for Divit MindSpace

Ready-to-paste templates. Update names/links/dates as needed; keep the overall
pattern tight — decades of research show short asks outperform long ones.

---

## 1. WhatsApp / SMS review ask — after first consultation

Send 24–48 hours after the consultation. Short, specific, one link.

### For parents of a child client

> Hi {{ParentName}}, this is {{SpecialistFirstName}} from Divit MindSpace.
> It was lovely meeting you and {{ChildName}} on {{Date}}. If it felt helpful,
> would you take 30 seconds to share a Google review? It genuinely helps
> other Bangalore families find us. 🙏
>
> {{GoogleReviewLink}}

### For adult clients

> Hi {{ClientName}}, this is {{SpecialistFirstName}} from Divit MindSpace.
> Thank you for reaching out earlier this week. If today's session was useful,
> would you share a short Google review? Your words help others in Bangalore
> who are in a similar place right now.
>
> {{GoogleReviewLink}}

### Follow-up (7 days later, only if no review yet)

Send ONCE and then stop. Never nag.

> Hi {{Name}} — checking in from Divit MindSpace. If writing a review slipped
> through, no worries at all. If today's a quieter moment, here's the link:
> {{GoogleReviewLink}} 💚

### Review link — how to generate

1. Go to your Google Business Profile manager (business.google.com)
2. Click **Get more reviews** → **Share review form**
3. Copy the short link — looks like `https://g.page/r/Cd.../review`
4. Save it somewhere obvious (notion / team WhatsApp pinned message)

## 2. HARO / Qwoted / Featured.com reply template

You'll get emails 3×/day with journalist queries. Filter by relevant topics:
autism, ADHD, child development, mental health, physiotherapy, Indian
healthcare, parenting. Reply ONLY when you can give a concrete, expert answer
— journalists sniff out generic replies in seconds.

### The high-win template

**Subject:** Quote — {{topic}} — Dr. {{LastName}}, Clinical Psychologist

Hi {{JournalistFirstName}},

I'm {{FullName}}, {{Title}} at Divit MindSpace, Bangalore — a center for
mental health, neurodevelopment, and physiotherapy. Happy to help on your
{{topic}} piece.

**Quote (use as-is or adapt):**

"{{SpecificInsight}}. {{SupportingContext}}. {{ActionableTakeaway}}."

**Why I can speak on this:**
- {{Credential1}} — e.g., Psy.D, 20+ years in clinical practice
- {{Credential2}} — e.g., Founder of Divit MindSpace, serving families in Bangalore
- {{SpecificExperience}} — e.g., Led 500+ ADHD assessments across children and adults

**Happy to:** expand via email, jump on a 10-min call, or send a photo.

Warmly,
{{FullName}}
{{Title}} · Divit MindSpace · Kasavanahalli, Bangalore
{{PhoneOrEmail}}
https://divitmindspace.com/specialists/{{SpecialistSlug}}

---

### HARO win rates go up dramatically when:
1. **Reply within 1 hour** of the query going out (inbox is flooded after ~2 hours)
2. **Give the quote first**, credentials second — journalists copy-paste the quote
3. **Specificity** — "1 in 44 US children" > "many children"; name cities/institutions; cite stats
4. **Include a link to the specialist's page on our site** (backlink opportunity)
5. **Offer a photo** — 2× more likely to be chosen

### HARO categories to subscribe to:
- Health & Fitness
- Lifestyle & Fitness
- Biotech and Healthcare
- General

Sources:
- HARO (helpareporter.com) — free, US-heavy but Indian journalists use it too
- Qwoted — similar, free tier
- Featured.com — Indian + global, $0–$29/mo
- SourceBottle — Australia + India

## 3. Post-workshop follow-up (for schools, corporates, community groups)

Send within 24 hours of delivering a workshop.

> Hi {{OrganizerName}}, thank you for having us at {{Venue}} yesterday.
> It was a real privilege to talk to your {{Audience}} about {{Topic}}.
>
> As promised, here's our {{ResourceLink}} — feel free to share it widely.
>
> If anyone from today wanted to book a free consultation, we're at
> https://divitmindspace.com/contact-us — or WhatsApp us at +91 99016 66139.
>
> Would you consider adding Divit MindSpace to your school/company resource
> list? Even a one-line mention on your website helps other families find us.
>
> Warmly,
> {{SpecialistName}}

**Why this works:** asks for a backlink (resource-list mention) in a
natural, value-first way. Schools routinely link to specialist centers
they've worked with — you just have to ask.

## 4. Review response templates (respond to EVERY Google review within 24h)

### 5-star review — positive

> Thank you, {{ReviewerFirstName}}. It was a real privilege to work with
> you{{ AndFamilyIfApplicable }}. We're so glad {{SpecificDetail — "the
> assessments brought clarity" / "the sessions have helped" / etc.}}.
> Wishing you continued progress — and please know we're always here if
> you need us. — {{SpecialistFirstName}} & the Divit MindSpace team

### 4-star review

> Thank you, {{ReviewerFirstName}}, for taking the time to share this.
> We're glad {{PositivePoint}} worked for you. We've noted your feedback
> on {{ConcernPoint}} — if you'd like to talk through it directly,
> please reach us at +91 99016 66139 or divitmindspace@gmail.com.
> Your feedback is how we keep improving. — Divit MindSpace team

### 3-star or lower — only reply publicly once, then take it offline

> Thank you, {{ReviewerFirstName}}, for the honest feedback. We take this
> seriously and would like to understand better what happened. Please
> reach out to {{SpecialistName}} directly at +91 99016 66139 or
> divitmindspace@gmail.com so we can make this right. — Divit MindSpace

**Rules:**
- Never argue on Google Reviews publicly
- Never share clinical/session specifics — HIPAA/privacy equivalent
- Always invite an offline conversation
- Fix the underlying issue; a fixed complaint often becomes a 5-star follow-up
